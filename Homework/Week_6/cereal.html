Nutritional values of 50 different cereal bars

Amber Nobel - 11819359


Cerels are a popular type of breakfast all over the world. It is a tasty and crunchy start of the day. But this raises the question, is this start of the day also a healthy start. To answer this question, I made you some visualiation tools. I took 50 well-known cereal brand and gathered their nutritional values per 100 gram. By clicking on the bar chart on the prefered ceral brand, you can see a pie chart that will give you more information on the amount of fat, proteins and carbohydrates in that specific ceral brand.
